HDFC PAY

The well-known Indian bank, HDFC, has extensive services available for payments and collections for businesses in the country. So if you are an Indian business, HDFC can be a great partner to handle your online and offline payment needs. HDFC offers digital online payments, mobile phone payments.

Key Features

Integration

HDFC payment gateway can also integrate with existing applications and support different shopping cart applications for a smoother and faster checkout experience.The HDFC payment gateway supports all major credit, debit cards and net banking in India, including Visa, MasterCard, Visa Electron, Diners, Rupay, Discover or Maestro. It also allows for existing HDFC customers to pay through EMI for the purchase when applicable

Accept Payments

The settlement system with HDFC is in real-time and settled into the business account. So once the payment is authorized and validated, the business account will be immediately credited with the purchase amount, less any deductions. As a business, you can also log into your HDFC business dashboard where you can see transaction data, reports and real-time updates on payments

Scale Up Your Online Business

HDFC offers international payments for your customers that allows them to make the purchase in their home currency. HDFC accepts 16 international currencies and is MasterCard/Visa compliant. While this is an attractive feature, what is often not explained clearly is the exchange rate that your customers get on their purchase.
